5/5 Chihuahuas are the cutest little dogs with their miniature bodies, distinctive pointy ears and big round eyes. They are just made for cuddles and would sit curled up on your lap all day!

4/5 They may be small but they have a BIG personality! They are extremely devoted, loyal dogs, who are full of courage!

3/5 Chihuahuas are very alert and are quick learners. They are eager to please, especially with positive reinforcement.

3/5 Chihuahuas are a very athletic, energetic little breed. They are natural entertainers, who love to amuse their owners with their playful antics.

5/5 Chihuahuas are known to be quite vocal and can bark A LOT! Especially when they feel anxious, excited or threatened. Their bark definitely makes up for their size!
